Yucatán Adventure

  • Day 1: Arrival in Merida - Transfer to your boutique hotel. Welcome cocktail reception.
  • Day 2: Merida City Tour - Explore the colonial architecture, markets, and local cuisine.
  • Day 3: Uxmal and Kabah - Guided tour of these lesser-known yet stunning Mayan sites.
  • Day 4: Celestun Biosphere Reserve - Flamingo watching and a boat tour through mangroves.
  • Day 5: Chichen Itza - Full-day tour of the iconic archaeological site, including a traditional Yucatecan lunch.
  • Day 6: Valladolid and Cenotes - Discover this charming town and swim in crystal-clear cenotes.
  • Day 7: Izamal - Visit the “Yellow City” with its striking convent and pyramid.
  • Day 8: Rio Lagartos - Boat tour to see crocodiles, flamingos, and other wildlife.
  • Day 9: Beach Day in Progreso - Relax on the Gulf coast, enjoy seafood, and beach activities.
  • Day 10: Departure - Transfer to Merida Airport.
